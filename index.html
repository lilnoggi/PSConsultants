About Rob

As a strategic-thinking CFO, I have extensive experience leading and expanding group finance organisations for well-established international companies in Professional Services, Retail, E-commerce, and Private Equity. Throughout my 20+ global finance career, I have built solid success in creating and executing strategic financial plans, as well as instituting robust financial governance structures to expand high-growth businesses and bolster financial profitability. As a highly-regarded Board-level Finance Executive Leader, I have informed and shaped the strategic decision-making process on mergers and acquisitions, investment selection, business restructuring, and profit optimization. With a proven track record of success as a Business Transformation Leader, I have executed company-wide/ group-wide change management and restructuring plans to return businesses to profitability or to prepare them for IPO/Sale, Mergers, Acquisition, and Integration, or BPO/Shared Services establishments.
